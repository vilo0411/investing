# Anti-AI Content Rules

Goal: Eliminate generic "AI-vibe" and ensure high E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).

## 1. Banned Phrases (Low Value / Robotic)
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "A journey into..."
- "Unlock the potential..."
- "It is important to note that..."
- "In conclusion..." (Use a call to action or final thought instead)
- "Deep dive into..."

## 2. Structural Rules
- **No Bullet Point Overload:** Don't just list things. Explain the "How" and "Why" between points.
- **Action-First:** Start paragraphs with verbs or specific examples.
- **Short Sentences:** Break down complex logic into punchy, readable sentences.

## 3. Human-Touch Elements
- **Personal Pronouns:** Use "We," "You," "I" where appropriate to build rapport.
- **Real-World Examples:** Every claim must be followed by a concrete example or industry data point.
- **Counter-Intuitive Insights:** Don't just state the obvious. Provide "contrarian" or "insider" views.
